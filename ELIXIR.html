Soft Drinks

Our soft drinks at ELIXIR are an exquisite symphony of taste, offering a range of five distinct and irresistible flavors.

  1. Citrus Splash:

    Embark on a vibrant journey with our Citrus Splash soft drink. This zesty blend combines the tangy notes of sun-kissed oranges, lemons, and a hint of grapefruit, creating a refreshing explosion of citrus goodness with every sip.
    • Flavor Profile: A lively combination of sun-kissed oranges, lemons, and a hint of grapefruit.
    • Tasting Experience: Experience a refreshing burst of citrusy goodness with every sip. The perfect balance of sweet and tangy notes invigorates your palate, making Citrus Splash an ideal choice for those seeking a vibrant and uplifting beverage.

    • Flavor Profile: An exotic blend of juicy pineapple, succulent mango, and a touch of coconut.

Fruit Juices

  1. Mango Tango Splash:

    • Ingredients: Pure mango nectar, a splash of passion fruit, and a hint of lime.
    • Tasting Experience: Dive into the tropical allure of ripe mangoes combined with the exotic twist of passion fruit. The addition of lime adds a zesty kick, creating a Mango Tango Splash that is both sweet and invigorating.

  2. Berry Medley Burst:

    • Ingredients: A blend of succulent strawberries, blueberries, blackberries, and a touch of mint.
    • Tasting Experience: Immerse yourself in the rich tapestry of berries with a burst of freshness from mint. Berry Medley Burst is a symphony of sweet and slightly tart notes, offering a delightful and revitalizing fruit drink.

  3. Watermelon Wave Quencher:

    • Ingredients: Juicy watermelon chunks, a hint of cucumber, and a splash of lime.
    • Tasting Experience: Ride the wave of refreshment with the hydrating essence of watermelon. The addition of cucumber and lime creates a cooling effect, making Watermelon Wave Quencher a perfect choice for hot summer days.

  4. Pineapple Paradise Punch:

    • Ingredients: Pineapple juice, coconut water, and a blend of tropical fruits (papaya, guava, and mango).
    • Tasting Experience: Transport yourself to a tropical paradise with the sweet and tropical fusion of pineapple, coconut, papaya, guava, and mango. Pineapple Paradise Punch is a true escape in a bottle.

  5. Citrus Breeze Bliss:

    • Ingredients: A medley of oranges, grapefruits, and lemons with a touch of honey.
    • Tasting Experience: Enjoy the crisp and refreshing sensation of a Citrus Breeze Bliss. The combination of citrus fruits harmonizes with a hint of honey, creating a citrusy symphony that is both invigorating and subtly sweet.

    6. These fruit drinks are crafted to provide a diverse range of flavors, catering to various preferences and offering a burst of natural goodness with every sip. Whether you prefer tropical, berry-infused, or citrusy delights, this variety of fruit drinks has something to tantalize every taste bud.
